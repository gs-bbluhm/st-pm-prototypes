What is an Analyst Agent

Agents that curate exactly the context you need to act.

An Analyst Agent is a team of specialized AI with one job: to continuously curate the ideal picture of each customer. Where they are, what they need right now, and the move that works, kept current as the relationship changes and ready before your team even asks.
